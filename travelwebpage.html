More Info

The Gardens at Terra Xotique are filled with various beautiful flowers that are great for photography and for posing with! Our gift shop provides seed packets of all your favorite species if you'd like a piece of Terra Xotique to take home with you. You can also purchase faux flowers to take back with you if you don't have a green thumb.

The Pools of Terra Xotique are surrounded by beautiful foliage to provide shade on sunny days. We've got a deeper pool and even a children's area where kids can have fun safely. Cool off afterwards in one of the showers and followed up with a massage!


Back to top

Surfing at Terra Xotique is not only fun, but very safe! We've got both flowriders as well as cushioned surfing simulators for everyone to have fun on. When you feel like you're ready to catch a bigger wave, we have instructors who will guide you in the nearby ocean.
